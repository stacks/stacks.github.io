What is it?

There will be another in-person Stacks Project Workshop in the near future. The 2020 Stacks Project Workshop will be virtual!

The setup of the Stacks Project Workshop will be similar to the previous edition (more information on the 2017 SPW), where graduate students, postdocs and senior mathematicians collaborate on a topic in algebraic geometry in small groups.

The list of currently confirmed mentors is:

  • Jarod Alper
  • Johan de Jong
  • Max Lieblich
  • Martin Olsson
  • Alex Perry
  • Ravi Vakil
  • Kirsten Wickelgren

More information will be available in due time.

Date and location

The workshop will be online from August 3 to August 7, 2020.

Applications are now closed.

Note: If you are accepted to the workshop, we will later ask for your preferences for group/topic/mentor.
