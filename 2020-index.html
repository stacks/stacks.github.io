What is it?

There will be a new Stacks Project Workshop (more information on the previous version), with a setup similar to the previous edition, where graduate students, postdocs and senior mathematicians collaborate on a topic in algebraic geometry in small groups. This iteration of the workshop will have a more arithmetic focus.

The list of currently confirmed mentors is:

  • Johan de Jong
  • Max Lieblich
  • Martin Olsson
  • Rachel Pries
  • Joe Rabinoff
  • Lenny Taelman
  • Ravi Vakil
  • Kirsten Wickelgren

More information will be available in due time.

Date and location

The workshop will be at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, from August 3 to August 7, 2020.
