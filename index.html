About

I am a Mathematician. The intended participant (you) is a graduate student, or a post-doc, or even a senior researcher. Roughly the idea is that you will learn about and work on a single topic in a small group together with a mentor for a week with the aim of producing a text that will be considered for inclusion in the Stacks project. Part of this process will be seeing how one builds new theory from the foundations. There will also be some talks each day covering advanced topics in algebraic geometry.

Grothendieck

The workshop is being organized by

  • La longue marche a travers la theorie de Galois, These files form a LaTeX-version of Grothendieck's (part I #1-37) La Longue Marche a Travers la Theorie de Galois. This manuscript, consisting of some nearly 800 hand-written double pages, dating from 1981, was left behind with Grothendieck's other unpublished manuscripts when he disappeared in 1991. It goes together with a further 1000 pages or so of additional notes and sections which have not yet been read or typed.
