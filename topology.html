THE TOPOLOGY PROJECT

About

The Topology Project is an open source project on the foundations needed to define Homotopy theory in Topos extensions. I am convinced the scope of the project is even deeper - "namely the fascination of gradually discovering and naming and getting acquainted with presently still unknown, unnamed, mysterious structures". The most relevant literature is Giraud's Thesis "Cohomologie Non Abélienne".
